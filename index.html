About

Hi, my name is Nicholas Nisbet and I'm an actor/writer from Penrith NSW. I've been pursuing acting for several years, and have worked on several large productions such as 'Thor: Love and Thunder', 'Wolf Like Me', and 'The Commons'. As well as some smaller productions such as 'Before 8am' and 'Urchin' which is currently doing the international film festival circuit. In this time I have gained valuble skills and knowledge about acting and filmmaking from experienced people in the industry
When I'm not acting, I'm an avid writer, and have written countless scripts including feature length films, short films, and tv shows. I'm very passionate about writing and strive to make great stories. My love for writing inspired me to move into filmmaking, and my first short Film "Time" was a semi-finalist in the 'Phoenix Short Film Festival 2021'
